Cross-chain asset management

Manage Relaychain & Parachain assets, anywhere, anytime.

Users can add assets, support Relaychain and Parachain to transfer, receive, and view the transfer history and state. Have the visual asset change analysis chart, make it easier for users to analyze assets. Users are notified when they receive the asset and can view the transfer details data.

The private key is self-owned, and have the Gesture,Fingerprint, Facial recognition, Hot and cold wallet mechanism, users can set their own scheme. Our team is developing a new encryption scheme -- high - dimensional fractal encryption, will be used to safeguard the security of a user using the polkawallet.

Intuitive staking passage

Command the whole view of, everything about staking is under control.

Validators: These participants will play a crucial role in adding new blocks to the Relay Chain and, by extension to all parachains. Any instances of non-compliance with the consensus algorithms result in punishment by removal of some or all of the validator’s staked DOTs, Good performance, however, will be rewarded.

Nominators: these participants may nominate validators to carry out validation work on their behalf. Bonding DOTs to a validator requires nominators to perform due diligence and constant analysis in order.

Polkawallet makes it easier for validators and nominal ators to make their contributions, by making the charts more intuitive and having a detailed history of each validators, for better analysis and research.

Convenient governance

Never miss an opportunity to participate in governance.

Polkadot uses a sophisticated governance mechanism that allows it to evolve gracefully over time at the ultimate behest of its stakeholders.Referenda are simple, inclusive, stake-based voting schemes. Each referendum has a specific proposal associated.

Polkawallet provides a more intuitive and convenient entry point for participating in governance. If there is a new referendum/proposals, the user is reminded and you can view the details. Users can governance directly from polkawallet and view the history governance records. So polkawallet also improves public Referenda engagement.

Interchain Communication

Cross-chain asset transfer is just a side application

Polkadot is enabling interoperability between chains, regardless of their features or their status as a private or public chain. Interoperability lets diverse chains perform arbitrary messaging, including value. This interconnectivity could encompass privacy-oriented projects, forks, permissioned chains and more.

Polkawallet will follow in Polkadot's footsteps and continue to expand the cross-chain ecosystem, which is a module for the near future. It will quickly integrate suitable cross-chain applications, and we believe that it will be a colorful page. Cross-chain asset exchange is just a side application.
